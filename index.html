Shape Royale Beta

Shape Royale is a game where you have to survive as long as you can in a battle royale. You can play as a square, circle or triangle.

Shape Royale is currently in beta and is not finished yet. It is currently only available on PC.

Shape Royale is made by Plazma Studios.

About Shape Royale

The Modes

Shape Royale has 2 different modes: Battle Royale against the cpu and a quest / level mode where you work through the game in a series of levels. (Note: These features are far from complete)

Battle Royale

In the battle royale gamemode you spawn in a random position and have to survive until you are the last player standing.

Player Selection

When you start the game you can choose to play as a square, circle or triangle. Each shape has different stats and abilities.

Square

The square is the most balanced shape in the game. It has very standard stats however it will never fail you!

    • Health: 4 hits
      Speed: Normal
      Rate of fire: 1 shot per 0.5 seconds

    • Circle

    The circle is the most defensive shape. It it has a very low rate of fire and speed, however it has a high ammount of health!

    • Health: 6 hits
      Speed: Slow
      Rate of fire: 1 shot per 0.75 seconds

    • Triangle

    The triangle is the most offensive shape. It has a very high rate of fire and speed, however it has a low ammount of health!

    • Health: 3 hits
      Speed: Fast
      Rate of fire: 1 shot per 0.4 seconds

    • Levels

    In the levels gamemode you work through a series of levels. Each level has a different objective and a different map. (Note: This feature is far from complete) Each level has a different layout, some have enemys some don't. There is also a wide variety of enemys all matching the same stats as the player shapes. To complete a level you work through the objectives until you get to the yellow flag.

    Level 1

    Level 1 is a basic level. It has no enemys and is just a basic level to get you used to the game.

    Level 2

    Level 2 is very similar to level 1. It has no real difficulty.

    Level 3

    Level 3 is a bit more difficult than the previous levels. It intoduces the square enemy and is a bit more challenging to complete.

    Enemys

    There are 3 different types of enemys in the game. Each enemy has the same stats as the player shapes.
